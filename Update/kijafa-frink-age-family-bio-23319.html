    Rose to fame in 2012 after she married former NFL quarterback Michael Vick. She has earned over 200,000 followers on her kijafa Instagram account, where she posts fashionable outfits and pictures of her family. 

    Before Fame

    She and Vick met at a nightclub in 2002 just a year into his NFL career.

    Trivia

    A fashion aficionado, she founded her own fashion website Pnkelephant.com and launched the hair extension brand MyHairCloset.com.

    Family Life

    Together, she and Michael Vick have two daughters, London and Jada, and a son named Mitez.

    Associated With

    Some of Vick's NFL friends attended their wedding such as LeSean McCoy, Andy Reid, and Plaxico Burress, amongst others.
