    Gospel singer who is known as one of the original members of gospel group Zie'l, and shares the accomplishment of two time Stellar Award Nominee. She rose to fame for the song she wrote for the group, "State of Emergency" that reached top 15 on Billboard. Her Instagram account keyondralockett has gained over 30,000 followers. 

    Before Fame

    In the Summer of 2012, she released her first solo project Soul Couture I the mixtape.

    Trivia

    She is a co-founder of the clothing brand, Jolie Noire apparel that has been featured in ESSENCE and Cosmopolitan. In October 2016, she inked a toy doll deal with The KenyaÂ® Doll Brand, an African-American brand. She uses major streaming platforms like Spotify to release music, and by May of 2023 her audience on the platform had risen to more than 25,000 monthly listeners. A handful of her songs on Spotify have received hundreds of thousands of views.

    Family Life

    Her sister and business partner is Kimberly Lockett. Their mother is Ronda Gray. 

    Associated With

    She has shared the stage with artists such as Yolanda Adams, Karen Clark-Sheard, Kierra Sheard, The Clark Sisters and Kirk Franklin.
