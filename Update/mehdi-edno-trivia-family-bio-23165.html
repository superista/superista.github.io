    Reality star who rose to fame as one of the original housemates on the summer edition of the popular reality competition and dating series Love Island, Season 10, which takes place in Mallorca. He has also earned over 60,000 followers on his mehdiedno Instagram account, which features images from his modeling shoots as well as travel photos and selfies. 

    Before Fame

    He worked as a communications manager and earned his master's degree before joining the Love Island villa. He also worked as a part-time model. 

    Trivia

    He was initially coupled up with Ruchee Gurung until the series' 1st re-coupling on Day 5 when he was chosen to pair up with bombshell Whitney Adebayo. He remained with Whitney until Day 23, when he was dropped from the Love Island villa in a surprise double-elimination, along with Mal Nicol. 

    Family Life

    He was born and raised in Bordeaux, in the southwestern region of France. 

    Associated With

    He appeared on the summer edition of Love Island Series 10, along with castmates Molly Marsh, Tyrique Hyde, and Ella Barnes. The series was hosted by Maya Jama.
