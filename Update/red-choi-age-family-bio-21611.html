    TikTok star known for his comedy sketches about Asian culture and music covers. Many of his TikToks get over a million views, and he has earned a following of 5 million on Tiktok and 1.2 million Instagram followers. 

    Before Fame

    He attended UC Berkeley and majored in English. 

    Trivia

    One of his popular videos was a comedy sketch about an introvert asking if he could hang out with his friend. Posted on February 5th, 2021, it earned over 11.8 million views and 2.8 million likes. 

    Family Life

    He showed his father on his Instagram in February 2021. He dated Gina Darling from December 2020 to March 2021. 

    Associated With

    In January 2021, he posted a trilingual cover of the song "Monster" by Justin Bieber and Shawn Mendes.
