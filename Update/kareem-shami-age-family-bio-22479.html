    Social media personality and TikTok content creator known for his self-improvement messages and positive content on his syrianpsycho account. He encourages others to improve their mindset. His messages caught on, and he quickly built a following on social media. He has more than 1.2 million followers on the platform.

    Before Fame

    His original TikTok account was banned at 300,000 fans, and it was dedicated to Arab and Muslim-related content. He was started a new account in late 2021. He spent 19 years in the Middle East before going to Law School. He graduated from university in late 2020 and then came to the US to pursue his dreams.

    Trivia

    He makes skits about his Muslim heritage. He occasionally shares fitness videos from the gym. In May of 2022, his "maximize your potential" video went viral with more than 1.5 million views. He also posts skits about classic movies, as well as motivational videos. 

    Family Life

    His girlfriend makes appearances in his content. He has two brothers and a sister. His mother has also appeared in his videos. He is from Syria, which he and his family fled because of war. Despite these challenges, he remained determined to make a better life for himself and his loved ones. He resides in Los Angeles, California.

    Associated With

    He created a TikTok set to a soundbite of Dr. Dre from the early 1990s.
