    DJ, singer and social star who became a resident DJ at Amnesia in 2017. She started a YouTube Channel called Chloejanesworld where she posts about music, fashion, and makeup. Her Instagram boasts to more than 100,000 followers. 

    Before Fame

    She started dancing at age 6 and playing piano at age 9. She grew up going to Ibiza, spending her summers watching DJs perform.

    Trivia

    She performed in Ibiza at KM5 during the summer of 2016 and spun at Charity Events hosted by Paris Hilton. She realized her dream of becoming a DJ at age 12.

    Family Life

    Her family is filled with professional musicians. 

    Associated With

    DJ John Benitez inspired her to go to the DJ school Dubspot.
