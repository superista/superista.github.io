    Popular social media star who gained popularity for the body-positive dance videos and duets she posts to her siennamae TikTok account. She has accumulated over 13 million followers on the app. She has a second TikTok account under the username siennamaegomezz and she is also known on social media by the name spicylatina. She is also well known on her siennamaegomez Instagram account, where she has amassed over 1.8 million followers for content such as her "Girl Likes to Eat" series.  

    Before Fame

    She launched her Instagram account in March 2016. Her first picture was an image from a rural part of Arizona. Before gaining fame on social media, she was a competition dancer in her hometown of Temecula, California.

    Trivia

    She earned over 7 million views on an August 2020 TikTok POV video where she hangs out on a set of bleachers with two friends. The video is set to the song "Forever" from HBO's Euphoria. In September 2020, she was featured by TikTok as one of their 12 Latinx Trailblazers. She has her own swimwear line. She has appeared in campaigns with brands such as Maybelline, Levi's, IPSY, Converse and more.

    Family Life

    Her parents' names are Ramón and Dina and they launched their own TikTok accounts under the usernames siennamaesdad and siennamaesmom. Her brother Jacub is a photographer. She is the second youngest of four children. Her siblings in addition to Jacub are Micaela and Caden. She and her family moved to Los Angeles to further her career. She lives part time in Hawaii. She and her sister Micaela did community theatre together as children.

    Associated With

    In August 2017, she posted a picture alongside R&B artist Khalid to her Instagram account. In August 2020, she collaborated on a TikTok video with fellow TikToker Jaden Barba, with whom she grew up dancing. She is close friends with creators Sommer Ray and Summer McKeen. She appeared in a 2021 music video alongside Becky G, Julia Michaels and Khea.
