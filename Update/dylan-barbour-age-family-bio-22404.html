    Reality television star known for competing on the fifteenth season of the dating series The Bachelorette. Known for his shy but funny side, he is also known for his passion for cooking and boats and shares photos of both through his Instagram for his over 550,000 followers. 

    Before Fame

    Prior to his television debut, he attended Williams College where he graduated in 2016. He then started his career as a tech entrepreneur, having founded the Vizer exercise app in 2018. 

    Trivia

    In June of 2019, it was announced that he will also participate in an edition of Bachelor in Paradise. 

    Family Life

    In 2019, he got engaged to Hannah Godwin. He often shares remembrances of his father who passed away from cancer through his Instagram. 

    Associated With

    He shared a photo with The Bachelorette season 15 contestant Hannah Brown to his Instagram in July of 2019.
