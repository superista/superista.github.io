    VH1 reality television personality from the series Love & Hip Hop: Hollywood who has also worked as a model and actress.

    Before Fame

    She was working as a model before premiering on the second season of Love & Hip Hop: Hollywood as a recurring cast member.

    Trivia

    She has an incredibly strong following on Instagram, accumulating over 800,000 followers.

    Family Life

    She was born in Puerto Rico and raised in Los Angeles, California. She previously dated rapper Lil Fizz. She began dating NBA shooting guard Bradley Beal in 2015; they had their first baby in 2018. She revealed their engagement in a January 2020 Instagram post. They were married later that year.

    Associated With

    She is a fan of the music of Toni Braxton and frequently mentions her on Twitter.
