    YouTuber and gamer known for her love of scary video games. She also likes ghosts, ghouls, and cosplay. She said she had a weekly ritual of, "talking absolute nonsense and sometimes playing scary games." She has earned more than 170,000 subscribers on her Kristycherise channel.

    Before Fame

    She joined YouTube in July of 2013.

    Trivia

    On her Twitch account, she has streamed games like Mortuary Assistant, Old School RuneScape, and Far Cry: Primal. In March of 2020, she shared photos from the Nara and Fushimi Inari-taisha shrine in Japan.

    Family Life

    She is originally from Australia.

    Associated With

    She visited the Hobbiton movie set in New Zealand, a location used for Peter Jackson's The Lord of the Rings: The Fellowship of the Ring trilogy and The Hobbit: An Unexpected Journey trilogy.
