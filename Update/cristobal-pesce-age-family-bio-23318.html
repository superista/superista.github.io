    Content creator on YouTube who would earn 430,000 subscribers to his main channel by posting a variety of types of fun videos. Additionally, he would create a channel called El Chico De Los Brownies to post videos about desserts. The latter channel surpassed 100,000 subscribers in March of 2018.

    Before Fame

    He registered for what would become his desserts channel in June of 2013 under the username of ColombaPR about one month before launching his to-be main channel.

    Trivia

    The first Instagram pictures he posted to his account were from a vacation in Chile.

    Family Life

    He was born in Chile but has lived in Colombia since he was young. He was in a relationship with Juan Pablo Jaramillo.

    Associated With

    Xtiian Zech was featured in one of his more watched videos, "ROAST VS ROAST."
