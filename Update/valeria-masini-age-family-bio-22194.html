    Puerto Rican model known through her valeriamasinipr Instagram page where she has amassed over 160,000 followers. She mostly posts fashion modeling photography and some of her traveling. She has also worked as an anchor on Guerreros Puerto Rico, an athletic competition show.

    Before Fame

    She made her first post to Instagram on April 29, 2019.

    Trivia

    She placed Top 5 in Miss Universe Puerto Rico. Besides modelling, she is also a pole-vaulting athlete. In April 2022, she attended Coachella, and posted photos of her time there on Instagram.

    Family Life

    She was born in Puerto Rico.

    Associated With

    She and Edwin Burgos are both Puerto Rican influencers.
