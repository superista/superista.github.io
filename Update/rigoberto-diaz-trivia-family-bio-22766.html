    Rap artist best known by his stage name Young Drummer Boy. He gained recognition for his 2017 song "Can't Tell Me Nothing," which has been streamed over 5.4 million times globally. According to Spotify, he has accumulated over 145,500 monthly listeners.

    Before Fame

    He began his professional music career in 2018. His debut single, "Blue'd Up," was officially released on April 21, 2018.

    Trivia

    Just behind his song "Can't Tell Me Nothing," his second most popular song, "Coke Dealer," was streamed over 3.5 million times on Spotify. He has posted seven different songs on Spotify that have all been streamed over 1 million times. 

    Family Life

    He grew up living with his family in Pomona, California. He was born of Mexican descent. 

    Associated With

    He and King Lil G collaborated together to make the 2019 song "Lights Out."
