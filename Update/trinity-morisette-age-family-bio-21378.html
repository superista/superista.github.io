    Positivity influencer on social media, in addition to model and video creator, who became most recognized for her content posted to her trinity TikTok channel. She has earned more than 3.9 million fans. She has also posted multiple outfit pictures and workout videos for her 650,000 followers on her trinity_morisette Instagram account. 

    Before Fame

    At the age of 14, she was accepted into a rigorous college program at Florida Atlantic University. She became a pre-health student, dual majoring in neuroscience and biology. 

    Trivia

    She took a stand against cyberbullying and pioneered the "Kindness Matters" Movement after receiving massive amounts of hateful comments on one of her videos. She worked for the Salvation Army in 2021. 

    Family Life

    Her older brother Gino has Down syndrome, and Trinity cited him as one of her inspirations. She also has an older sister named Payton. 

    Associated With

    She made a collaborative TikTok video captioned as "HOW DID I GET SO LUCKY! LOVE these GIRLS... TAG UR BESTIES" which featured Datrie, Daryna Popach and Catherine Rowley.
