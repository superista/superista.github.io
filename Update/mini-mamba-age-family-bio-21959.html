    YouTube star and gamer who frequently appears in the videos of his older brother Diamond Mamba. He is also featured on the Reaxtion channel and has helped it earn over 56 million views. The channel has prominently featured Fortnite battles and challenges. 

    Before Fame

    He was given his own custom Team SoloMid gaming jersey in August 2018.

    Trivia

    A significant number of his videos have been about the game Call of Duty: Black Ops 3. In a December 2020 video he showed off his gaming setup, which he claimed was worth of 20 thousand dollars. 

    Family Life

    His older brother is YouTube personality Diamond Mamba. His other brother is YouTube personality reaxtion.

    Associated With

    He rose to fame on YouTube thanks in part to his posts involving Black Ops 3 like TooShade.
