    One-half of the duo Emily and Evelyn alongside her older sister Emily. They have accumulated more than 1.8 million subscribers by posting toy openings, skits and challenges. They have sold their own colorful clothing on their emilyandevelyn.com website. 

    Before Fame

    The Emily and Evelyn YouTube channel was created in January 2015; it was previously called TwoSistersToyStyle.

    Trivia

    Some of Emily and Evelyn's most popular videos, such as "FIN FUN MERMAID TAILS - REAL MERMAIDS IN OUR POOL - Dream Come True Becoming a Mermaid!" and "FIN FUN MERMAID TAILS - Clown Fish and Blue Tang Mermaids - Disney Finding Dory Robo Fish Water Toys" have earned more than 3 million views apiece. She has also done multiple videos of herself making and trying different foods. 

    Family Life

    She and Emily are Florida natives, but later relocated to Los Angeles, California.

    Associated With

    She gained fame as a YouTube toy vlogger, much like Daisy's Toy Vlog.
