    Family member known for being the daughter of Helena Bonham Carter and Tim Burton. She is also already an actress, having appeared as a young girl in a flash back in the 2010 feature film Alice in Wonderland directed by her father. 

    Before Fame

    She was born in 2007 and her name was officially reveled in 2008. She then made her first film appearance in 2010 at the age of three. 

    Trivia

    Her name Nell was inspired after all the Helens in Helena Bonham Carter's family according to an article publishing in the Jewish Journal in 2008. 

    Family Life

    She was born and raised in London, England and has a brother named Billy Raymond Burton. 

    Associated With

    Anais Gallagher is a fellow celebrity daughter who was also photographed at the Primrose Hill Fair in November of 2016.
