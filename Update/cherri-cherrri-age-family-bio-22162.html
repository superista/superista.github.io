    TikTok dance star known for her account which has amassed a whopping 10 million followers. She is the main character on her channel that she shares with her husband who they call Greapa while she goes by Cherri. Together they do comedy videos with exaggerated, aberrant humor of the silliest variety. He will occasionally join her for dancing videos, but for the most part, she chooses to shine alone while doing her interpretation of trendy K-Pop dances. 

    Before Fame

    She and her partner have been together for more than half a decade and seem to genuinely enjoy producing their wild and silly content. Ever since the birth of their son, they often include him in their videos as well. He has learned from his parents and has great comedic timing. 

    Trivia

    She and her partner's YouTube channel has earned more than 110,000 followers for their misadventures. The channel provides Greapa more space, as he has his own playlist of vlog videos. 

    Family Life

    Cherri and Greapa have been together for many years and he finally proposed, as they announced on their TikTok channel in 2021. Their son Chae Dol was born on the 20th of October in 2019. They have two dogs and a cat. 

    Associated With

    On June 2022 she posted a dance video featuring her song as they both rocked out to the K-Pop band NCT.
