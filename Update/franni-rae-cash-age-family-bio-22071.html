    Gospel singer and member of Christian music group We the Kingdom. Their album's titular song, "Holy Water" received a 2021 Grammy nomination for Best Contemporary Christian Music Performance/Song. Her Instagram account frannirae, which currently has 20,000 followers, features posts about her personal and professional life, as well as pictures with friends, her dog, and her husband. 

    Before Fame

    Before forming We the Kingdom, she endured a difficult point in her life in which she struggled with self-harm and severe self-criticism after experiencing a situation of religious and emotional abuse. She often speaks out about mental health and her journey of recovery by creating music. She grew up surrounded by music and began writing songs as a child and for her father and uncle as an adult before becoming a singer and performer as well. 

    Trivia

    She is related to the majority of her bandmates. The only member of We the Kingdom to whom she is not blood-related is longtime family friend Andrew Bergthold. 

    Family Life

    She married singer Austin Cain in July 2020. She is a member of the band We the Kingdom, along with her father Ed Cash, brother Martin Cash, and uncle Scott Cash. She says that her mother cannot sing. She also has a dog named Cowboy. 

    Associated With

    She posted a photo alongside actress and podcast host Hannah Billingsley in November of 2022, with whom she is a good friend. That same month, she uploaded a video of herself dancing with her husband to the song "I'm Yours" by Jason Mraz. The couple is good friends with former NFL player EJ Gaines.
