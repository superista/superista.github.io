    Magician and entertainer who has performed card magic all around the world. He has performed for celebrities such as Bruce Willis, Michael Bolton, Tom Holland, and Rosie Perez. He has earned more than 900,000 followers on his cardmagicbyjason TikTok account. 

    Before Fame

    He started learning magic at the age of seven. He learned about ten to fifteen card tricks that all used some basic sleight of hand. 

    Trivia

    He specializes in corporate events, trade shows, and private parties for Fortune 500 companies. His clients have included General Electric, New York Yankees, Verizon, and Time Warner Cable. He performed on shows including ABC's 20/20 and Inside Edition. 

    Family Life

    His mother took him to see David Copperfield perform at the Palace Theater in Albany, New York. As a child, he performed for his parents and older brother. 

    Associated With

    In March 2023, he was featured on Jennifer Hudson's talk show.
