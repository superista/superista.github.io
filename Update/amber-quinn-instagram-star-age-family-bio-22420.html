    Entrepreneur, fitness and business and crypto mentor, and social media influencer who has earned over 700,000 followers to her amberquinnofficial Instagram account. She is a brand ambassador for Fashion Nova, Pretty Little Thing, Matte Collection, Maltepe Dental Clinic, and Q Sciences. 

    Before Fame

    Her experiences with financial difficulty growing up inspired her to never have to live paycheck to paycheck and become the entrepreneur, influencer, and businesswoman that she is today.

    Trivia

    She worked in a hair salon for two years, but realized it was not for her because she wanted to work from home and travel. She worked as a model from a young age and even competed in the 2017 National All-Star Twin Peaks Bikini Contest hosted by DSTYLE Magazine.

    Family Life

    She grew up in a single parent household which often struggled financially and led them to, at times, live with friends or in their car while homeless. She was raised by her mother. A photo of her as a baby with her mother can be seen in the "My Journey" series of photos on her Instagram account.

    Associated With

    She posted a video to her amberquinnofficial TikTok account in March 2023 using the song "Love" by Keyshia Cole.
