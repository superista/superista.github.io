    YouTube star who rose to fame for the rap music that she makes with her older sister Jazzy. They post their music videos, pranks, skits, and vlogs on their The Lit Sisters YouTube account and have over 320,000 subscribers. She posts selfies and lifestyle pictures on her amourdezy_ Instagram and has over 220,000 followers. 

    Before Fame

    She posted her first YouTube video in December 2019 with Jazzy. In the video, she and her sister vlog about their older sister Zarria's birthday. 

    Trivia

     In July 2020, she and Jazzy released their "Lit Sister Anthem" music video featuring their younger brother Kd Da Kid on their The Lit Sisters YouTube channel. The video went viral earning over 3 million views. Her most popular YouTube video titled "Dezy gets stuck on the scariest water slide lol" has been viewed over 9 million times.

    Family Life

    She frequently makes content with her older sister Jazzy. She has three sisters and two brothers. Her parents, Dez and Jay, have a YouTube channel under the name of The Lit Family. She also has a younger brother who has a popular YouTube channel under the name of KD Da Kid & Da Gang. 

    Associated With

    She and her sister have made videos with Kinigra Deon.
