    Actress known for playing secretary Francesca Liddy in the series Breaking Bad and Better Call Saul. She was in the 2019 film To the Stars, the 2020 film Minari and the 2022 film Land of Gold. She was in the podcast series Welcome to Night Vale.

    Before Fame

    Her first film role was in 2000's Dr. T & the Women starring Richard Gere.

    Trivia

    She appeared in series like Walker, Texas Ranger, Lone Star, Dallas and Longmire. In 2006, she was in the television movie The Year Without a Santa Claus. In 2010, her voice featured in an episode of the Breaking Bad: Original Minisodes. So far in her acting career, she has made over 50 appearances in different films and TV series'.

    Family Life

    She is from the United States.

    Associated With

    Her costars in Better Call Saul included Bob Odenkirk.
