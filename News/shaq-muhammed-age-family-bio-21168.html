    Reality star famous for appearing on the winter series of Love Island's 9th season. He was an original housemate of the villa, entering on Day 1 and making it all the way to the final episode of the show, where he and partner Tanya Manhenga finished in 4th place. He posts images of his travels and accomplishments to his shaq24s_ Instagram page, which currently has 210,000 followers. 

    Before Fame

    He earned his college degree in 2020, before becoming an airport security guard.

    Trivia

    He won several challenges during his time in the Love Island villa, including "Raunchy Races" in Week 5 and the sports day challenge in Week 7.

    Family Life

    His mother Polite and his sister Tahira both appeared on the penultimate episode of Love Island to share their thoughts with him on his relationship with Tanya. 

    Associated With

    He appeared on Love Island with fellow contestants Olivia Hawkins and Ron Hall. The show was hosted by Maya Jama.
