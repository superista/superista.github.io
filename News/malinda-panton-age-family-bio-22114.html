    YouTube vlogger who is widely known for her Panton's Squad channel. She has gained popularity there for her couples' pranks and challenges alongside her husband Don Panton. She has also been featured in an original music video on her family account. 

    Before Fame

    She first met her husband Don in 2006. She helped co-create their YouTube channel in January 2017. 

    Trivia

    Her YouTube channel has surpassed 2.6 million subscribers and growing. In 2021, she got a blue Lamborghini Urus and posted a photo of it with a bow on it to her dimplez_103 Instagram account. 

    Family Life

    She and her husband Don have children named Sariyah, known online as Yaya, and DJ. In May 2021, she gave birth to their third child, a son named Bryson.

    Associated With

    She featured YouTube star Damis Squad Ahzee in a YouTube video on her channel in October 2020.
