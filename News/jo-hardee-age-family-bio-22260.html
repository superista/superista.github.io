    Reality star known for appearing on Lifetime's Kim of Queens from January 2014 to April 2015. She was the mother of the show's star, pageant coach Kim Gravel.

    Before Fame

    She worked at her daughter's business, Pageant Place.

    Trivia

    She often had conflict with the pageant mothers in the series.

    Family Life

    She has daughters named Kim Gravel and Allisyn.

    Associated With

    She is one of many reality stars who appeared in pageant series, including Lacey-Mae Mason.
