    TikTok star who is known for posting beauty content that includes make-up product reviews, tutorials, and vlogs about her lifestyle. She often tries out new products based on fan recommendations. Her TikTok account sarah_wolak has gained over 1.6 million followers.

    Before Fame

    She attended St. Lucy's High School. She was captain of her cheer team. After graduating, she attended the Fashion Institute of Design and Merchandising and received her associates in Beauty Marketing and Product Development.

    Trivia

    One of her most viewed TikToks, with over 5.6 million views features her showing a lipstick case shaped like a guitar, with a lipstick that applies three different shades of red. She got her bachelor's degree from California Baptist University and then moved out to Nashville, Tennessee. She has also become known for her lip stain videos, as well as some of her random lip combos and makeup challenges, reviews, and vlogs. 

    Family Life

    She has a sister named Mary Wolak. She is in a relationship with Jackson Mackliff, her high school sweetheart. She is from Los Angeles. She also has a Malteze Shitshu named Milton. 

    Associated With

    In December 2022, she posted a photo with Selena Gomez.
