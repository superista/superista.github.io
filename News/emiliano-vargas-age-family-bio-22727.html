    The son of former boxing champion Fernando Vargas. He spent 8 years boxing in the amateur circuit before turning professional in 2022. He gained fame on his personal emilianofvargas Instagram account for his boxing content. 

    Before Fame

    He joined his family in the 2014 reality show Welcome to Los Vargas.

    Trivia

    Taking after his father, he has appeared in YouTube videos exhibiting his own boxing skills. His professional debut was announced for May 14, 2022, in Los Angeles.

    Family Life

    His mother's name is Martha Lopez Vargas. His brother Amado Vargas is also a professional boxer. 

    Associated With

    His father appeared in the 2006 feature film Alpha Dog alongside Emile Hirsch.
