    Fashion model and social media star who famously appeared on the cover of Harper's Bazaar Brasil in November 2019. She has been represented by Vision Los Angeles, Iconic Management, Women 360 in Paris, and Elite Model Management. She has modeled for well-known fashion brands such as Veronica Beard, Deity, Etam, and Tony Ward Haute Couture. 

    Before Fame

    She made her professional runway debut at the Balmain Spring/Summer Fashion Show in 2020. 

    Trivia

    Many of her modeling photos can be seen on her nataliekuckenburg Instagram account, which boasts more than 70,000 followers. One of her most popular Instagram reels features her on a balcony in Paris, France. It has earned over 200,000 views. 

    Family Life

    She has a sister named Nasti and a brother named Dennis. She has been dating The Vampire Diaries star Paul Wesley since November 2022. 

    Associated With

    She walked the runway for designer Chiara Boni at New York Fashion Week in September 2022.
