    English TikTok personality known as the father of social media star Jamie Nyland. He is known for posting comedy content about his daily life. Some of his most viewed videos feature him dancing to popular music by artists like Ciara and Megan Thee Stallion. He has amassed more than 280,000 followers and 2.6 million likes on his richard.nyland account. 

    Before Fame

    He launched his TikTok account in 2019. In his first video, he introduced himself as "the daddy of TikTok." 

    Trivia

    In January 2021, he appeared in a TikTok video that went viral. In the video, his son surprised him by paying off his mortgage so he could retire early. When he asked why he did that, his son responded "because I love you". 

    Family Life

    He lived in Gloucestershire. He posted videos in which he played pranks on his brother. He is also the father of Shaun Nyland.

    Associated With

    He and Watson Hirschfield are both famous English TikTok stars who post comedy content.
