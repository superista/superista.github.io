    Reality star who is best known for her role on the hit reality franchise Below Deck. She was on seasons four and five of Below Deck Mediterranean and season one of Below Deck: Down Under. In 2023, she appeared on the reality show I'm a Celebrity...Get Me Out of Here Australia. 

    Before Fame

    She earned a double science degree from Victoria University in Wellington, New Zealand. After working in a kiwifruit lab following graduation, she decided to look for work on a yacht alongside her sister. 

    Trivia

    She worked on yachts for five years and was then approached about joining the Below Deck family. Her aesha_jean Instagram account has garnered 800,000 followers where she posts career highlights and photos from her daily life. In July 2022, she posted a paid partnership with Smirnoff. 

    Family Life

    She grew up a middle child in a family of six children. Her parents divorced when she was young. She has siblings with mental health issues and a brother who died from a brain tumor. She is in a relationship with Scotty Dobbo. 

    Associated With

    She starred alongside Hannah Ferrier on the reality show Below Deck Mediterranean.
