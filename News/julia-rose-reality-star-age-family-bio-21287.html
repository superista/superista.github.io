    It was through a series of failed relationship with unfaithful partners that she decided to join the cast of Are You the One?

    Trivia

    In September of 2016, she and Stephen McHugh opened the joint YouTube channel Jules & Stephen John where they posted the debut video "001."

    Family Life

    She briefly dated Jake Paul in 2020. She previously dated her Are You the One? co-star Stephen McHugh. 

    Associated With

    Other cast members on the fourth season of Are You the One? include Asaf Goren, Kaylen Zahara, and Prosper Muna.
