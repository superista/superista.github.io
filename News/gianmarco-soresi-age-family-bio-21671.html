    New York based stand-up comedian, actor and creator known for his appearances on television shows like Blue Bloods and BONDiNG. He is the winner of season eight of Amazon's Comics Watching Comics. He garnered further acclaim from NPR, Esquire, The Atlantic, Buzzfeed, ABC News, and many more. He's also performed stand-up on The Late Late Show with James Corden, Comedy Central, Don't Tell, The Real Housewives of New York, and was selected as a JFL New Face in 2022. He went on to appear in the multi-comic standup series on Netflix called Verified Standup in 2023.

    Before Fame

    He earned a BFA degree in musical theatre from the University of Miami.

    Trivia

    He posts clips from his stand-up comedy performances on his gianmarcosoresi TikTok account, which has amassed over 600,000 followers. He has also acted in Billy Crystal's Here Today, Netflix's Bonding, Hustlers, The Last OG, and is the host of the popular podcast "The Downside with Gianmarco Soresi."

    Family Life

    He has been in a relationship with Tovah Silbermann.

    Associated With

    Billy Crystal and Dierdre Friel were his fellow cast members in the 2021 comedy-drama film Here Today.
