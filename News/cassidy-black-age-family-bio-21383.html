    Wife of country star Dierks Bentley.  Their marriage in 2005 came in the form of an elopement in Mexico three days after he proposed to her. Dierks' song "Woman, Amen" was written about her. 

    Before Fame

    She and Dierks met in the eighth grade. 

    Trivia

    They reconnected and began dating after she showed up at his tour bus after he performed as the opening acct for George Strait at a show in Las Vegas. She helped name his 2018 album, The Mountain. She raised $20,000 for the Safe Haven Family Shelter in 2017 by running the Boston Marathon. 

    Family Life

    She and Dierks have three children. 

    Associated With

    Elvie Shane, Dustin Lynch, and DJ Aydammn toured with her husband in 2022.
