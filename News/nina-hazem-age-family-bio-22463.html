    TikTok mom who is best known for sharing a variety of lip sync and dance performances on her self-titled account. She has more than 2.3 million followers on the platform. She is the mother of popular TikTok and Instagram star keemokazi. 

    Before Fame

    She began posting TikToks regularly in July of 2020. One of her earliest videos is set to Major Lazer and Justin Bieber's "Cold Water."

    Trivia

    In early 2021, she and her family rented the famed Invisible House in Joshua Tree. She wore a Dia de los Muertos themed Halloween costume.

    Family Life

    Her children, Sophie, Sara, Serene, Saby, Sima, and Kareem, also known as Keemokazi, appear in nearly all of her videos. 

    Associated With

    She and her family performed a TikTok dance to Jason Derulo's "Savage Love" in late 2020.
