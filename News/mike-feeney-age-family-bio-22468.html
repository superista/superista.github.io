    Stand-up comedian whose humorous videos have helped him earn over 500,000 followers to his iammikefeeney TikTok account. He has performed on numerous widely known talk shows, including The Tonight Show with Jimmy Fallon and NBC's Last Call with Carson Daly. He currently performs in New York City at The Comedy Cellar, New York Comedy Club, and Gotham Comedy Club.

    Before Fame

    His debut album, Rage Against the Routine, debuted in 2019, and it premiered at #1 on the iTunes Comedy Charts.

    Trivia

    He won "Best Micro Short" at the Houston Comedy Film Festival. The Irish Goodbye Podcast, which he co-hosts, was featured on the Top 100 Apple Comedy Podcasts. His TikTok page, which has helped him rack up more than 16 million total likes, features segments of some of his stand-up sets. His TikToks routinely receive hundreds of thousands of views.

    Family Life

    He married wife Erica Brustman in 2015. They became the parents of Leo James Feeney in November 2022.

    Associated With

    On December 16, 2022, he appeared on The Tonight Show with Jimmy Fallon along with guests James Corden and Jenna Ortega.
