    Oldest of the Yeager family, this YouTube star appears on the channel Shot of The Yeagers alongside his family. The channel has earned more than 5 million subscribers with over 3.6 billion views. It has featured games, challenges and other family oriented comedic content. 

    Before Fame

    He was nine years old when his parents created the channel. 

    Trivia

    In October they posted a special Q&A with Stephen called "A SHOT OF STEPHEN | Q&A SPECIAL." He has his own self-titled YouTube channel. He has also played football for his school team. 

    Family Life

    The oldest child of Jamie and Steve Yeager, his younger siblings include Taylor, Payton, Jordyn, Parker and Blake. When he was just a year old his parents welcomed their second child Bryce who passed away in childbirth.

    Associated With

    He is a YouTube star like EvanTubeHD.
