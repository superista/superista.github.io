    Actress who rose to stardom after playing Kim in Disney XD's Kickin' It and starring in the 2012 TV movie Girl Vs. Monster. In 2018, she began starring as Dagger in the Freeform series Cloak & Dagger. Also a singer, she won a Radio Disney Music Award for "Had Me @ Hello" in 2013. 

    Before Fame

    She appeared in commercials for Mattel, Kidz Bop 14 and Hasbro as a child, and practiced cheerleading and gymnastics. 

    Trivia

    She recorded part of the playlist for Girl Vs. Monster, titled Make Your Mark: Ultimate Playlist, and sang a cover of "Winter Wonderland" for the 2012 Disney Channel Holiday Playlist album.

    Family Life

    She was raised by Mark and Kim Holt in Germantown, Tennessee. She has a sister named Morgan Toll and a brother named Cade Holt. Owen Holt is her cousin. She dated Ray Kearin from 2015 until 2017.

    Associated With

    In 2012, she co-starred with Luke Benward in the family comedy film Girl Vs. Monster.
