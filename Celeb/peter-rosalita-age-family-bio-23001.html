    Singer who has gained fame for his performance of "All By Myself" by Eric Carmen on America's Got Talent. The video has been viewed over 3 million times on YouTube. Another video of him singing "I Have Nothing" by Whitney Houston has gained over 2 million views on YouTube. 

    Before Fame

    He began competing as a singer when he was six years old. He attended Richmindale School and was trained at the Cadenza Music Institute

    Trivia

    He competed in the Indywood Talent Hunt 2019 UAE Chapter. He covered a song by Mariah Carey on America's Got Talent in the summer of 2021.

    Family Life

    His father's name is Ruel and is a chef. His mother's name is Vilma and is a cashier. His mother manages his social media accounts. His aunt Mary Jane Villegas accompanied him to the televised audition. He is Philipino. 

    Associated With

    Pop singer Celine Dion, who did a famous version of "All By Myself," reacted to his performance on a YouTube video.
