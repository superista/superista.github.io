    Actor known for playing Kyle Graham in the series Superficial Intelligence. He was series including Dead To Me, Better Things, Nicky, Ricky, Dicky & Dawn and Nashville. He executive produced and starred in the series Bloodlines.

    Before Fame

    He earned a Bachelor's in Fine Arts in Theatre Studies and Art & Technology from Youngstown State University and the Art Institute of Pittsburgh.

    Trivia

    He has had roles in series like Justified, Franklin & Bash and The Fosters. He appeared in the soap opera Days of Our Lives. He was in television movies including Open Marriage and A Dangerous Defense.

    Family Life

    His father is a singer and his mother is an artist. 

    Associated With

    He was in the 2021 film F.E.A.R. which starred Marci Miller and Danny Ruiz.
