    Hip-hop artist and rapper who went viral with the 2019 hit "Crayon Money." He managed to find an audience through his kddakid5 Instagram account and KD DaKid YouTube channel.

    Before Fame

    In April 2019, a video of him rapping on an airplane appeared online. 

    Trivia

    The music video for his single "Puppy Love" was viewed more than 24 million times. He did his first music collaboration with Grey Skye on a song called "Hands Up." The music video has gained over 5 million views.

    Family Life

    His social media accounts were monitored by parents. He has four sisters. His sisters Jazzyy and Dezy have popular Instagram accounts at thereal_jazzyy and amourdezy_, respectively. He also has sisters named Zarria and Nihya.

    Associated With

    He posed for a photo with fellow rapper Supa Peach in late 2019.
