    One of the younger members of the Nelson family that featured on the channel Not Enough Nelsons. They have earned more than 1.3 million subscribers for their channel by posting challenges, skits, and adventure entries.

    Before Fame

    Her family launched their channel with a video called "15 SIBLINGS?! I'M NO LONGER A PART OF THIS FAMILY! | Big Family Youtube Channel" in December of 2018.

    Trivia

    She is the sixth youngest in her family. Her siblings are DeLayNee, PresLee Grace, ElleCee, Beckham, Ledger, Luke, NayVee, SaiDee, LiLee, KassaDee, JourNee, KennaDee, and Jaine Nelson. She was in the 2019 "MORNING ROUTINE with 16 KiDS!!" video, which has received over 4.5 million views since it was released. 

    Family Life

    She was born in Utah. Her parents are Tiffany and Benji.

    Associated With

    Her oldest siblings are KassaDee and KennaDee.
