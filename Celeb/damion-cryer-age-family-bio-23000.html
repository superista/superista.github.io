    Mukbang video creator who also shares family vlogs and reaction videos on his channel, The Cryer Family. The channel has earned more than 500,000 subscribers. His mukbang videos have included tacos, seafood boils, turkey and chicken wings. 

    Before Fame

    His first YouTube videos showcased his boat and some of his fishing adventures.

    Trivia

    His son Damien Prince runs the popular YouTube channels The Prince Family and D&B Nation alongside his wife Biannca Raines. Damion has appeared in videos on both channels. He has posted multiple photos of winning scratch off games on his Instagram page. 

    Family Life

    In addition to his son Damien, he has another son named Darion, as well as a son who was born in December 2020.

    Associated With

    His most watched video became "Dad Reacts to RiceGum - God Church (Official Music Video)."
