    Forward who began her collegiate career at the University of Oregon before transferring to play for the University of Notre Dame Fighting Irish. She was also a member of the 2019 USA World Cup Team. She has accumulated more than 20,000 followers on her kylee.watsonn Instagram page as a result of her sports fame. 

    Before Fame

    She played basketball for Mainland High in Linwood, New Jersey. She won numerous awards and emerged as a standout and top player. 

    Trivia

    As a child, she played soccer, volleyball, and track in addition to playing basketball. She attended the same high school that both of her parents attended. 

    Family Life

    She was born and raised in Linwood, New Jersey. She is the oldest of three siblings. Her father Tim Watson played college football before playing for the Seattle Seahawks in 2000. Her mother Courtney Neall played college basketball and was senior captain for her college team. 

    Associated With

    She became a star player for the Fighting Irish like Skylar Diggins and Marina Mabrey when they played for the team.
