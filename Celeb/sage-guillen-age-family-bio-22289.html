    TikTok content creator and social media personality best known for his sage_37 account. He shares lip-sync performances and relatable messages using the platform's text-on-screen effect. He has more than 210,000 followers on the platform. He also is part of the podcast Suburb Talks.

    Before Fame

    He made his TikTok debut in early 2021 with a collaborative dance set to the viral Tyler April sound.  

    Trivia

    In September of 2022, one of his TikToks went viral with more than 1 million combined views. His TikToks have generated more than 8 million likes in total. Fans can find him on Instagram under the handle thesageguillen. His TikToks regularly achieve hundreds of thousands of views. 

    Family Life

    He was born in the United States. He has a pet Yorkie.

    Associated With

    He created a TikTok set to music made famous by Ski Mask the Slump God.
