    Most well known as the eldest daughter of YouTube vlogger Bramty Juliette. She has collaborated with UGGs kids and Rags to Raches. A video posted in March of 2019 showed her competing in her first beauty pageant. 

    Before Fame

    She made her YouTube debut in the April 2016 YouTube video "SPRING LOOKBOOK 2016." 

    Trivia

    She has her own self-titled Instagram account, which has earned more than 340,000 followers. Her Instagram account has featured multiple photos with her siblings and has been managed by her mom. 

    Family Life

    Her father, Luis Espina, also appears with her on her mother's self-titled YouTube channel. She has twin siblings, Balcom and Levy.

    Associated With

    Like Hazzel Marie, she is the young star of an popular YouTube family vlogging channel.
