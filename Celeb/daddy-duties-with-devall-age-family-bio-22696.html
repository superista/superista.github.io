    Content creator known for posting parenting vlogs to his Daddy Duties with Devall YouTube channel. The family videos range from pranking one another to cooking food together. The account has gained over 280,000 subscribers. 

    Before Fame

    His first upload is from July of 2020. It centers around the family making tye-dye shirts. 

    Trivia

    One of the most popular vlogs follows an extremely stressful week for him. It has attracted over 400,000 views. 

    Family Life

    He has a wife and six children, all of which are the center of the YouTube channel. They gave birth to their sixth child in October of 2021.

    Associated With

    For the Super Bowl in 2021, when the Kansas City Chiefs played the Tampa Bay Buccaneers, he made a video for his YouTube channel showing off the food he made for then game.
