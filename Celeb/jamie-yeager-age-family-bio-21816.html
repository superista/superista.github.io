    18 Nov 2023, 00:00

    Rose to fame as the matriarch of the YouTube family vlogging channel Shot of The Yeagers. She and her husband, Steve Yeager, share the channel with their six children. Together, the family has built an audience of more than 6 million subscribers. 

    Before Fame

    The Shot of the Yeagers YouTube channel was created on August 9th, 2015. 

    Trivia

    One of the Yeager's most popular YouTube videos is "SHOTS, STITCHES AND SNOW CONES!!!" which earned more than 1 million views in less than six months. She has also been featured in multiple short form videos that were posted on the family's shot_of_the_yeagers Instagram page.

    Family Life

    She and Steve Yeager have six children: Stephen, Taylor, Payton, Jordyn, Parker and Blake. 

    Associated With

    Like Nia Rader, she is the mother of a popular YouTube family.
