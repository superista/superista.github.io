    Social media star and content creator who has gained popularity for the comedy videos she posts to her msbeanieofficial TikTok account, and the reaction videos she posts to her self-titled YouTube channel. She has earned over 3.8 million followers on her TikTok, where she is known for her comedic character Ms. Beanie, and over 2.3 million subscribers on her YouTube channel. 

    Before Fame

    She studied at California State University, Northridge, where she participated in theatre and acting competitions. She was an extra in the film Indivisible. She launched her YouTube channel and TikTok accounts in 2019.  

    Trivia

    She has two other TikTok accounts under jenniferkassir and arabjenn. One of her most popular TikToks, with over 6 million views, features her impersonating the rapper Tekashi 6IX9INE. She has appeared on several meme pages such as FunnyHoodz. 

    Family Life

    Her mom, Lina, is also popular on TikTok for her viral family skits. Her dad's name is Jay, and she has two siblings, Jaylene and Jeffrey. She is of Lebanese descent, and speaks Arabic.

    Associated With

    She posted a YouTube video reacting to a Diljit Dosanjh song in June 2019. She posted a Tiktok featuring the 6ix9ine and Nicki Minaj song "Trollz," which has earned over 1 million views.
