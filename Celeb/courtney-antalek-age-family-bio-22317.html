    Social media personality and TikTok content creator known for sharing lip sync and modeling videos on her courtneyyantalek account. She often makes GRWM content and records highlights from her day. She has more than 1.4 million followers on the platform.

    Before Fame

    She made her TikTok debut in late 2020. Her first video is captioned, "Vibin with my love."

    Trivia

    In early 2022, her "Little transition for the bday" video went viral with more than 6 million combined views. One of her most popular lip syncs has garnered more than 10 million views.

    Family Life

    She was born in New York.

    Associated With

    She created a TikTok set to Kid Cudi and MGMT's "Pursuit of Happiness." She has also featured music by The Beatles in her TikTok videos.
