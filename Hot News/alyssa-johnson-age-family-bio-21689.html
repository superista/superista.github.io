    Vlogger on YouTube and influencer on Instagram whose content features fellow personalities including Brooklyn and Bailey and Kesley LeRoy. 

    Before Fame

    One of her earliest YouTube videos was from August of 2015 and titled "BABY FOOD CHALLENGE!" 

    Trivia

    She and her family also run a joint channel called The Mikesell Family.

    Family Life

    She is the older sister of twins Brock and Boston. Her parents are Betsy and Gentry Mikesell. Her husband is Dallin Johnson; the couple got engaged in April 2021 and wed on June 18, 2021.

    Associated With

    She posted a video called "Come with me to a photoshoot with Kesley LeRoy."
