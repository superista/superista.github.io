    First-born child of platinum selling rapper Jeezy, who is known primarily for his 2005 studio album Let's Get It: Thug Motivation 101 which featured Billboard Hot 100 singles "Soul Survivor" and "And Then What." He is often referred to as "Lil Jeezy."

    Before Fame

    After graduating from high school, he enrolled at the Savannah College of Art and Design.

    Trivia

    His father is credited as being one of the most influential rappers in the origin of Atlanta's "Trap Music" era. 

    Family Life

    He grew up living with his parents in Atlanta, Georgia. 

    Associated With

    His father featured rapper Kanye West on the 2008 single "Put On."
