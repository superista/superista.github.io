    Social media phenomenon across YouTube, Instagram, and Twitter. He first gained a web following on the popular app Vine and as a member of the social media group Magcon Boys alongside Aaron Carpenter, Taylor Caniff, Jack Gilinsky, Shawn Mendes, Jacob Whitesides, and Jack Johnson. In 2016, he starred in the movie Be Somebody. 

    Before Fame

    He played football and lacrosse at Bishop Ireton High School. 

    Trivia

    His presence caused a minor riot at Tyson's Corner Mall in McLean, Virginia in September 2013. He showed up at VidCon in 2015 in a disguise, however his fans figured out it was him and he was quickly mobbed. 

    Family Life

    He was born and raised in Woodbridge, Virginia. He has a brother named Dylan, another brother named Ryan, and a sister named Kristen. His parents are named Rafael and Laura. He has been romantically linked to Olga Safari. 

    Associated With

    He has collaborated with fellow internet sensations such as Jc Caylen and Kian Lawley.
