    TikTok star commonly known as "the real-life Barbie doll." Her videos typically consist of makeup tutorials and getting-ready routines as well as tours of her Barbie-themed car, bedroom, etc. She consistently appears in each video donning sparkles, pink outfits, and other Barbie-like accessories. Her song "Money is Forever" was released on February 13, 2022, with artist Nuts6000. 

    Before Fame

    She created her thatdollmo TikTok account in July 2020. The account has since gained over 5 million followers and 208 million likes. 

    Trivia

    A video she uploaded to her TikTok account on March 12, 2023, with the caption "life is so unfair" earned over 2 million views within only 2 ½ weeks after being posted. She is known for promoting positivity, self-love, and confidence. From her car to her wardrobe and house, she has all things pink. 

    Family Life

    Her mother appeared in an October 2020 video posted to her TikTok account. She also has a sister named McKinley.

    Associated With

    Many of her videos refer to her similar appearance to Cassie from the television show Euphoria, played by Sydney Sweeney.
