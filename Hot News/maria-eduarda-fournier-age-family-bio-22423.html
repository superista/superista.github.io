    Wife of Brazilian soccer player Lucas Paquetá. Her personal Instagram, where she shares photos of herself and her family, has amassed over 2.5 million followers. She also posts promotional content for vitamin and health supplements as well photos of her playing volleyball. 

    Before Fame

    She first joined Instagram in May 2014. 

    Trivia

    She often goes by the nickname "Duda." In November and December of 2022, she posted many photos of her in Qatar supporting her husband as he played for Brazil in the World Cup. One of her most popular Instagram reels features her and her husband dancing. It has gained more than 4 million views. 

    Family Life

    She married Lucas in November 2018. They have lived in London with their two sons: Benício and Filippo. 

    Associated With

    She and Natalia Loewe Becker are both wives of famous Brazilian soccer players who competed in the 2022 World Cup.
