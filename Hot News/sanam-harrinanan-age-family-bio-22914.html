    Reality star who won season nine of Love Island with her partner Kai Fagan. She entered the villa on Day 29 and won the "Raunchy Races" challenge alongside the rest of the Main Villa. She entered the villa alongside Lynda Flix and Sammy James.

    Before Fame

    She worked as a social worker prior to her reality TV career.

    Trivia

    She has earned more than 430,000 followers on her sanamiee Instagram account. She also uploads videos for her growing 120,000 followers on TikTok. Her sanamiee TikTok page features clips from her personal life, including highlights from some of her travels. 

    Family Life

    She is from England. She has dated Kai Fagan.
