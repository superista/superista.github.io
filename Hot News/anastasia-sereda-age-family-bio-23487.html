    Ukrainian drummer who rose to fame for her drum covers of metal songs featured on her YouTube channel. Her drumming skills have brought her upwards of 370,000 subscribers. Outside of social media, she has played drums for the rock bands O.Torvald and Invertor.

    Before Fame

    She started drumming at the age of 16.

    Trivia

    Her an.drums Instagram account, which features video clips of her live performances and drum covers, has racked up more than 410,000 followers. She performed under the pseudonym Hebi when she was a member of O.Torvald. Over time, the content on her TikTok page has helped her receive more than 1.8 million total likes. Many of her video shorts on YouTube have earned hundreds of thousands of views.

    Family Life

    She is originally from Kiev, Ukraine.

    Associated With

    In a video posted to her YouTube channel on August 31, 2022, she was joined by Kristina Schiano for a cover of Paramore's "Decode."
