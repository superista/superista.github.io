    Canadian TikTok star famous for her powerful and soulful singing voice that she shows off in cover song videos, many of which are acapella. A video of her shocking her best friend by singing for her for the first time shared on January 7, 2022, has received over 21 million views. The video was reposted as a duet by the original artist behind the song she was singing, Paloma Faith, who said that Angela's voice sounds better than hers. 

    Before Fame

    She made her TikTok debut on December 30, 2019, with a singing video dedicated to the film Burlesque. 

    Trivia

    Christina Aguilera, Ariana Grande, and Jennifer Hudson are some of the artists she has covered. Her angelakhouryy_ TikTok account has gained 1.4 million followers and 30 million likes. On Spotify, she creates public playlists that promote the work of her musical influences. 

    Family Life

    She was born in Canada. 

    Associated With

    In one of her popular clips, she tests her vocal range by trying to sing "It's All Coming Back to Me Now" by Celine Dion .
