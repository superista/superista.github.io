    Hip-hop artist whose popular songs include "Rosa", "New Opp", "The Purge" and "Poppa Perry". He is known for being part of New York City's drill rap scene. He is also known for sampling popular songs and for his high-energy beats. He frequently performs live and features concert clips on his sha_gz Instagram account. He has amassed 250,000 followers.

    Before Fame

    He became active on Instagram in July 2022.

    Trivia

    In December of 2022, Rolling Stone profiled him and interviewed him about "New Opp", which was flagged and removed by YouTube because the music video depicted a minor smoking an unknown substance. In May 2023, he released the music video for his track "Better Off Dead", which came as an anticipated drop from his fans.

    Family Life

    He was born Shaheem Knott in the Bronx, New York. 

    Associated With

    His song, "New Opp", takes a sample from "Nuketown" by Ski Mask the Slump God.
